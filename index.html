NutriScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

NutriScan ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the NutriScan mobile application (the "App").

By using NutriScan, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information You Provide

When you create a profile or use certain features, we may collect:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Height and weight
  • Fitness goals (muscle gain, fat loss, general fitness)

This information is used only to personalize calorie limits, macro targets, and Fit Scores.

b) Usage and Device Information

We automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data (screens viewed, features used)
  • Crash logs and performance data

This data helps us improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

c) Barcode & Food Data

When you scan a product barcode, the app retrieves nutritional information from public food databases (such as Open Food Facts). We do not store images of barcodes or food packaging.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Calculate personalized calorie and macro recommendations
  • Generate goal-based Fit Scores
  • Enable food scanning and nutrition analysis
  • Improve app features, performance, and reliability
  • Communicate important updates related to the app

We do not sell or rent your personal data.
